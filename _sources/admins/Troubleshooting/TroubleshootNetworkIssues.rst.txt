Troubleshooting Network Issues
=====================================================================

.. include:: /shared/ProductLabels/CloudAndCustomerManaged_Label.rst

If you are having network issues, here are instructions to send a file with the data. 

1. Open your browser.
2. Clear your browser cache following the steps in: `Clearing Browser Cache and Cookies <https://kb.wisc.edu/helpdesk/page.php?id=12384>`_.
3. Click the menu icon in the upper right of your browser and go to **More Tools** > **Developer Tools**.

    .. image:: ../../_static/TroubleshootNetwork_Dev_Tools.png

4. Click the **Network** tab.

    .. image:: ../../_static/TroubleshootNetwork_Network_Tab.png

5. Log into your Alation instance.
6. Right-click in the table of events shown in the Network tab, and choose **Save as HAR with Content**:

    .. image:: ../../_static/TroubleshootNetwork_Save_HAR.png
