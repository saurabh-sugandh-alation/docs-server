=======================================
Customize Your Data Catalog
=======================================

.. include:: /shared/ProductLabels/CloudAndCustomerManaged_Label.rst

Alation catalog customization means creating custom fields, custom
article templates, and adding custom fields to both object templates and
custom article templates.

You need the role of the Catalog Admin or Server Admin to customize the
Alation catalog.
